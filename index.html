Digital Research Institute ENN Group

Research and Project

Xin'ao Xinzhi Digital Technology Research Institute is committed to the research of industrial Internet data intelligence and the transformation of enterprises into digital intelligence. In recent years, the Institute has focused on industry AI and collaborative learning core technologies, with fruitful R&D results.We have applied for more than 400 invention patents, published more than 10 high-quality papers and won awards in international conferences, and participated in the development of many technical standards.

Leading a team of 50 researchers and engineers. Focusing on Industrial IoT, Artificial Intelligence and Federate Learning research and development for smart energy and smart city application domains. Establishing collaborations among government agencies, companies and universities for Federated Learning platform development and interoperability, industry application deployment and use case demonstration, as well as technology standard releases.

🔥🔥Industry Artificial Intelligence
 Starting from the intelligent needs of the core business scenarios of ENN Group, we research multimodal data intelligence algorithms, including prediction algorithms based on time-series data, anomaly detection and fault diagnosis algorithms based on IOT data, computer vision algorithms based on image, video and special equipment imaging, cognitive intelligence algorithms based on Bayesian networks and knowledge graphs, and decision optimization algorithms based on operations research, bionics and reinforcement learning. At the same time, I am committed to research on cutting-edge AI technologies, including pre-training, migration learning and adaptive learning of industrial basic models, self-supervised learning, model compression and edge cloud collaborative deployment, as well as model interpretability and trusted AI.
🔥🔥Collaborative learning
 Under the premise of protecting data sovereignty and privacy security, jointly mining the value behind the data and building industrial intelligence ecology. Support a variety of machine learning and deep learning collaborative learning algorithms and adaptive model aggregation strategies. Using advanced AI technologies such as knowledge distillation, migration learning, self-supervised learning, difficult sample selection, and similar data clustering to reduce collaborative learning communication overhead, improve edge-cloud collaboration efficiency, and support ecological partners to use heterogeneous data, systems, and models to efficiently co-create high-quality AI models. Privacy protection technologies such as secure multi-party computing, homomorphic encryption, trusted execution environment and differential privacy are used to guarantee the security level while reducing the computation and communication overhead. The contribution of participants to the intelligent model is measured through the incentive mechanism technology, and the contribution of high-value data is encouraged to encourage industrial ecological mutual trust and cooperation, fair competition and sustainable development.
Relying on ENN Group's industry advantages in energy and other fields, we have established an industry-academia-research ecological cooperation system, an intelligent algorithm development and deployment platform and a cross-enterprise collaborative learning test platform. The results of the research and development have been experimentally verified and applied in the core application scenarios of the energy sector.
🔥🔥Gas Demand Prediction
 Using vertical collaborative learning to safely use customer production plans and industry knowledge, and horizontal collaborative learning compliance to access massive customer data from multiple city fuel companies. We have used techniques such as self-supervised learning for multivariate time-series data comparison to co-create an intelligent model for the load forecasting industry. It improves customer gas demand prediction accuracy and aggregation efficiency, and optimizes gas distribution operations and supply chain management.
🔥🔥Smart Community Security in Smart Home and City
Using collaborative learning technology security compliance to connect multiple data from property companies, gas companies, heating companies, government departments, etc., supporting joint privacy computing and joint industry modeling, providing data and algorithm basis for ecological cooperation. For example, we can master the living habits and accurate portraits of households to support more efficient security management, and train intelligent models to identify security hazards in households, public areas, and industrial and commercial households using multiparty picture and video data.
🔥🔥Equipment operation anomalies and fault diagnosis scenarios
Mechanistic analysis algorithms and artificial intelligence algorithms are combined to build a unified equipment operation and maintenance intelligent management system. We propose a collaborative learning framework for heterogeneous models with cloud-side collaboration, attracting factories, equipment manufacturers, sensor manufacturers, and university laboratories with different data and computing power resources, and building an open and inclusive cooperation ecology. This will solve the problems of scarcity of equipment failure data and difficulty in cold boot of intelligent applications, and improve the overall intelligence level of the industry.
